Texas Sports

Sports have always seemed to be a defining caracteristic of every culture, and Texas is no exception.

But we take sports to a new height, injecting it with our Texas pride.

The most serious of these sports would be the honored and practically worshipped sport of FOOTBALL! No, non of the soccer garbage. We're talkin' about REAL football. The one where each team is trying to pummel the other. Now THAT is a sport!

The two most revered teams, causing many family feuds and splitting up friends. Those teams are the University of Texas Longhorns and the Texas A&M Aggies.

So choose your team wisely...
